# About the Data

Dataset: [Real / Fake Job Posting Prediction](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shivamb/real-or-fake-fake-jobposting-prediction/data)

We are using a dataset that aggregates 18000 job postings, of which 800 are
phantom job postings. The real to fake job posting ratio is what makes the
classification problem interesting, being that most job postings are real. This
is why we believe this dataset is representative of postings on online job
platforms.
